I’m Zachary Harrison, a Donaghey Honors student at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock (UALR), studying Web Design and Development with a focus on UX design. I’m skilled in HTML, CSS, and design tools including Figma and Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign.

My passion for visual art, design, and technology naturally led me to this field, where I enjoy creating thoughtful, user-centered digital experiences.

I’ve worked on several website projects both individually and in collaborative teams, gaining experience as a designer, teammate, and project lead. I am continuing my studies at UALR while interning with USAble Life on their IT and Communications team, where I contribute to website design and troubleshooting communication technologies.
